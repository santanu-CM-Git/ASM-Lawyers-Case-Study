# ASM Lawyers — Legal Website Design & Development Case Study

## About Project

ASM Lawyers is a California-based employment and consumer law firm focused on protecting employee rights, workplace fairness, and consumer interests through litigation and class action representation.

The firm specializes in employment disputes, wage and hour claims, whistleblower protections, workplace discrimination, and consumer fraud matters.

This project involved designing and developing a modern, trustworthy, and conversion-focused legal website that communicates the firm’s expertise while making complex legal services approachable for employees and consumers seeking legal representation.

The objective was to establish stronger credibility, improve accessibility to legal information, simplify consultation inquiries, and deliver a professional digital experience aligned with the firm’s litigation-focused practice areas.

---

## The Client

ASM Lawyers is a boutique employment and consumer law firm with decades of combined legal experience representing clients across California.

The firm required a scalable digital platform capable of showcasing legal authority, attorney credibility, class action experience, and client-focused advocacy.

### Key Requirements

* Showcase multiple legal practice areas
* Present attorney profiles and case results
* Improve lead generation through consultation forms
* Build trust through testimonials and recognitions
* Simplify legal content for non-technical users
* Support long-form legal resources and case studies

The platform also needed to maintain a balance between professionalism, accessibility, and modern UX standards within a competitive legal industry.

---

# Problems

## 1. Legal Brand Positioning

The website required a clean and authoritative presentation capable of communicating complex employment and consumer law services in a user-friendly and trustworthy manner.

## 2. Complex Legal Content Architecture

ASM Lawyers manages multiple legal services and class action cases, requiring a scalable information architecture that allows users to navigate practice areas, attorney pages, case studies, and legal resources efficiently.

## 3. Low User Engagement & Conversion Flow

The previous digital experience lacked clear calls-to-action and optimized inquiry funnels, creating friction for potential clients initiating consultations.

## 4. Trust & Credibility Presentation

The platform needed stronger credibility indicators through:

* Testimonials
* Recognitions
* Legal achievements
* Attorney profiles
* Structured content hierarchy

## 5. Performance & Accessibility Issues

The website required improvements in:

* Page speed
* Responsive layouts
* Mobile usability
* Accessibility compliance

## 6. Content Scalability

The client required a flexible CMS system capable of managing:

* Legal content updates
* Blog publishing
* Attorney information
* Practice area pages
* Case-related resources

Without ongoing development dependency.

# The Solution

## Enterprise Legal Website Architecture

Designed a structured and scalable website layout capable of managing:
